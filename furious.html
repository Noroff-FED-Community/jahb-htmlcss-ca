about the game

Welcome to the world of "Furious"! A high-octane action game where you play as a futuristic bad-ass gorilla who's out to take down the corrupt corporate overlords who have enslaved his people.

As the main character, you'll navigate through a gritty urban landscape, using your incredible strength, agility, and weapons to take down hordes of enemies and complete dangerous missions. With a variety of high-tech weapons and gadgets at your disposal, you'll be able to customize your play style and approach each situation in your own unique way.

But be warned - the corporate overlords have a vast network of resources at their disposal, and they'll stop at nothing to take you down. From heavily armed guards to advanced security systems, you'll have to use all your skills and wits to outsmart your enemies and emerge victorious.

With stunning graphics, pulse-pounding action, and a gripping storyline, "Furious" is the ultimate gaming experience for anyone who loves fast-paced action and epic adventure. So strap on your armor, grab your weapons, and get ready to take on the world in "Furious" - the game that puts you in the shoes of a future bad-ass gorilla!
